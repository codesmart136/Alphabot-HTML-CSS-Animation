The ROADMAP
1. ACTIVATE BOTS
Phase one will begin with the activation of the Badbots. Activation will take place on the 15th of November.
The lovely people at Magic Eden have agreed to oversee the activation and make sure everything goes smoothly. You’ll be able to mint from their launchpad at 9PM EST.
2. AFTER ACTIVATION
Immediately after minting we will add 100 SOL to the community wallet and you will be able to trade your bots on the Magic Eden marketplace. We will also list the bots on Rarity Tools. Upon full minting, the Bot Factory will randomly drop 5 superrare bots into holder wallets. Exciting!
3. CHARITY INITIATIVES
We have selected three charities to support long term via the Brobots project. Blesma, SpecialEffect, and Revolution Robotics. Upon full minting, a $10,000 donation will be made to all three charities. View more info about the incredible work they do to change lives through technology & robotics.
4. COMMUNITY BUILDING
Following the launch phase, we will spend time and resources nurturing and growing our community. From rewarding our early adopters, to welcoming newcomers, we plan to ensure that the Brobots community is an exciting and inclusive one to be a part of for many years to come.
5. THE FUTURE
In future we plan to release a follow up to the original Brobots collection. We won’t give too much away, but we intend to explore a variety of concepts, and aim to build out Botworld with all sorts of cool and interesting characters and animals, not just Bros! Keep your eyes peeled!
